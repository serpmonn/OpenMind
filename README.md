   OpenMind is the first decentralized, open-source operating system designed specifically for humanoid robots. Our mission is to decouple robotic intelligence from proprietary hardware.
   Our First Milestone: "The Perfect Breakfast"
We are starting with the most complex household task: cooking eggs and bacon. This skill requires fine motor skills, object recognition, and real-time adaptation.
   Why OpenMind?
Hardware Agnostic: Designed to run on any humanoid (Tesla Optimus, Unitree, Figure, or DIY).
Collective Experience: Robots share "experience logs" via the cloud, learning from each other's successes and failures.
Open Standard: No corporate walls. The future of robotics belongs to everyone.
